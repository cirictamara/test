Whitepaper

Hi! We are the eCyber team and we're pleased to meet you.

In this whitepaper we will cover the details of eCyber NFT collection and our future plans.

What is eCyber Project?

eCyber consists of 3 interconnected NFT collections. It is an interactive comic book NFT project that has never been built on the blockchain network.

What is Interactive Comic Book?

It is a system where NFT holders participate in decisions that can directly affect the course of the story through weekly voting.

For example, at a critical point in the story, we will offer members of our community the option to decide the direction of the story. The next part of the comic will be shaped in line with the decision of the majority. In this way, the bond formed between our community and the brand we have created will increase in an unprecedented way with each published episode. After the story is finished online, the actual printing period will begin.

All of the income from the printed editions of our comic book, which ended in line with your decisions, will be delivered to the community through the Elrond Network. In the distribution of the income from the comics, a share will be taken at the rate of NFT you hold.

The Goal of the Project

  • To integrate the NFT ecosystem into daily life in line with every stated and realized goal.
  • To expand the usability of NFT's.

The Community Goals

  • Contribute to the Elrond and Holoride community by adding Egld / Ride liquidity which will be added to the Metastaking program in Maiar Exchange.
  • To build an NFT community and brand in line with our ideals and goals.

The goals after "Sold out"

  • 50% of the income from the Genl collection will be added to the Egld/Ride Metastaking and the LKMEX income will be shared equally among the community users (holders). (The same will apply to the Gen2 collection.)
  • Distribution dates of farm incomes will be determined by the community (NFT Holders).
  • LKMEX rewards will be harvested and transferred to LKMEX farm daily for maximizing the rewards in line with the interests of our NFT holders.
  • Earned RIDE token from Metastaking will be given to the community through our discord events.
